# Membership Overview

---

## Membership Costs

| Fee | Amount |
|-----|--------|
| Initiation Fee | $40 |
| Monthly Dues (Adults) | $40 |
| Monthly Dues (Juniors) | $30 |

## Requirements

A current USJF (United States Judo Federation) membership is necessary, with an annual fee of $70. The dojo can assist with USJF membership applications.

Beginners must purchase a uniform, available for purchase at the facility.

## Payment Method

Cash or check only.

## Visitors

Visitors are always welcome at the dojo.

For detailed information about starting at Seattle Dojo, see our [Getting Started](/getting-started/) page which offers an overview of the enrollment process.
