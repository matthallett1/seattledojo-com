## Directions

The facility sits 1 block south of Yesler Way and 2 blocks north of S. Jackson St, between 14th Ave S and 16th Ave S.

### From the East
Take I-90 tunnel exit, follow Rainier Ave S, turn onto 14th Ave S, then right onto S. Washington St. Look for the off-white building on the left.

### From the South
Via I-5 Dearborn Exit #164, through S. Dearborn St and Rainier Ave S, turning onto 14th Ave S, then S. Washington St.

### From the North
Via James Street Exit on I-5, following signs to Yesler Way, then turning onto 16th Ave S and S. Washington St to find the dojo.
