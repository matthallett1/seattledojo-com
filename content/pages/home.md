# Seattle Dojo - Homepage

**North America's First Judo Dojo**

---

## Welcome

Established in the early 1900s, Seattle Dojo represents the oldest judo facility in the United States. Operating from the same historic International District location (southeast of downtown Seattle) since the 1930s, the dojo offers classes throughout the week.

**Class Schedule:** Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Saturday, and Sunday

**Participants served:** The facility caters to diverse populations, from fitness-focused students to serious competitive athletes participating in local, regional, national, and international competitions.

**Youth Programs:** Dedicated classes teach judo fundamentals in an engaging setting, emphasizing physical coordination alongside self-confidence, discipline, and community connection.

**Visitor Information:** Please feel free to visit us during our regular practice sessions.
